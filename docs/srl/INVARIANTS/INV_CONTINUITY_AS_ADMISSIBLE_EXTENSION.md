# INV_CONTINUITY_EXTENSION — Continuity as Admissible Extension

## Statement

Continuity is the bounded relation among states or configurations across passage under which admissible ordering remains composable. Without continuity, composition across the passage is not supportable as one bounded relation.

Continuity does not by itself establish persistence or identity.

## Exclusions

Non-operative. Non-procedural. No test for continuity presence or absence. No threshold for sufficiency. No recovery procedure.
