## Prohibition statement

At CRL-0 / Stage-0, this program refuses to publish methods, procedures, algorithms, calibration steps, tuning rules, playbooks, scoring mechanics, enforcement mechanisms, or recipes of application.

## Applies to

- All CRL-0 surfaces (constraint-spine, CC website, Substack publications, foundation notes, program notes)
- All derivative projections including SRL

## Non-claims

- This refusal does not claim that methods do not exist.
- This refusal does not claim that methods cannot be developed.
- This refusal states only that methods are outside the scope of CRL-0 publication surfaces.
